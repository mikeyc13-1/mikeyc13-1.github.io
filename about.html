About Me

Your Court-Side Physio

Hi, I'm Mikey — a physiotherapist with a passion for keeping athletes on the court and performing at their best. I've spent years working with basketball players and coaches across Perth, from juniors through to NBL1.

I believe players deserve physio care that actually understands the sport; the demands, the movements, the culture. Generic rehab doesn't cut it for athletes who live and breathe the game.

Whether you're recovering from an ankle sprain, managing a chronic knee issue, or just want to move and warm up smarter, I'm here to help you get back to doing what you love, faster.
